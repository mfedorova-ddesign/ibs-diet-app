About IBS

Irritable bowel syndrome (IBS) affects the digestive system. Symptoms can include cramping, bloating, gas, and changes in bowel habits. Diet and lifestyle help manage symptoms.

Diet tips

  • Eat regular, smaller meals. Avoid large or fatty meals.
  • Many benefit from a low-FODMAP approach under guidance.
  • Limit caffeine, alcohol, spicy foods, and artificial sweeteners if they trigger you.
  • Increase fiber gradually; soluble fiber (e.g. oats) is often better tolerated.
  • Stay hydrated and keep a food diary to spot your triggers.

For inspiration only. Follow your doctor's or dietitian's advice.
